## Overview

MaypiVac helps pet owners register and manage vaccination data, consult campaigns, and navigate to vaccination points with integrated maps. It includes role management, real-time chat, and notifications.

## Key Features

- **Pet Records Management**  
- **Vaccination Campaigns**  
- **Real-Time Chat & Notifications**  
- **KML Map Handling**  

## Technologies Used

- **Flutter & Dart**  
- **Node.js & Express.js**  
- **MySQL & Firebase**  
- **Socket.IO**  

## Challenges and Learnings

Integrating real-time chat and mapping while maintaining smooth user experience required careful architecture.

## Outcome

MaypiVac provided an intuitive and reliable tool for pet health management, benefiting both users and administrators.
